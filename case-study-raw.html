Behind The Transformation

A Journey of Digital Repositioning

Despite having a premium product line formulated by top dermatologists, Lumina Skincare struggled to break through the noise of a saturated beauty market.

"We were burning cash on ads that didn't convert. Navora didn't just fix our ads; they fixed our entire approach to customer acquisition."

Sarah Chen

Founder & CEO, Lumina Skincare

Challenges

Low ROAS and Creative Fatigue

Few industries are as visual and fast-paced as skincare. For two years, the brand had been battling against giant conglomerates with massive budgets.

  • Fragmented Data: Attribution was broken across platforms, leading to double-counting and poor budget allocation.
  • Weak Creative Strategy: Ad creatives were beautiful but lacked the direct-response triggers needed to convert cold traffic.
  • Funnel Leaks: High traffic to the site wasn't converting due to a confusing checkout flow and lack of social proof.

Solution

A Full-Funnel Ecosystem

We didn't just patch the ads; we rebuilt the engine. Navora Partners implemented a "Full-Funnel Ecosystem" strategy designed to nurture customers from awareness to loyalty.

The 3-Pillar Strategy:

Creative Testing Framework: We launched 50+ creative variations in the first month, identifying winning hooks and angles.

Audience Segmentation: We moved away from broad targeting to interest-based clusters and high-value lookalikes.

Landing Page Optimization: We built dedicated landing pages for best-sellers that focused on benefits and social proof.

Results

Scaling with Confidence

The results were immediate and sustained. Lumina Skincare didn't just grow; they dominated their niche.

Year over year, revenue from paid channels skyrocketed. What started as a $50k/month channel became a $500k/month powerhouse.
