What Is The Growth Strategy?

The Growth Strategy is a complimentary, no-strings-attached deep dive into your brand's paid media and profitability. It's not a generic audit or a sales call in disguise — it's a structured analysis designed to surface the real constraints holding your growth back.


We look at what your ad platforms are reporting versus what your P&L actually shows. We identify the gaps, the waste, and the opportunities most teams miss because they're optimizing dashboards instead of profit.

What you'll walk away with…

✓ Clarity on where to invest and why
✓ P&L-aligned view of performance
✓ The constraints limiting growth
✓ A decision on whether partnering makes sense
WHAT THE PROCESS LOOKS LIKE

Step 1 — Leadership & Context Call

We start with a 30-minute conversation with your leadership team. The goal is to understand your business model, margins, growth goals, and what's been tried before — so our analysis is grounded in reality, not assumptions.

Step 2 — Metrics Review

We dig into your ad accounts, P&L, and attribution data. We compare what your platforms are reporting to what your bank account actually shows — and surface the gaps most brands don't even know exist.

Step 3 — Receive a Recorded Strategic Walkthrough

You'll get a detailed, recorded video walkthrough of our findings — including where we see waste, where we see opportunity, and what we'd do differently. It's yours to keep whether we work together or not.

Step 4 — Meet with our founder

Finally, you'll meet with Chaky to talk through the findings, ask questions, and decide together if there's a fit. No pressure, no hard close — just an honest conversation about what growth could look like.

Not a campaign critique, ad account audit or salespitch in disguise.

It's a way to determine if you're ready for scaling and if not, what to do to get there.
