What the process looks like

Step 1 - Leadership & Context Call

We start with a focused call to understand your business from the inside out. We ask about your growth history, margin structure, cash flow constraints, inventory, creative pipeline, and internal friction points so we're not just looking at data in isolation. Most growth issues aren't visible in the ad account alone. Context changes conclusions.

Step 2 - Metrics Review

We analyze your P&L, unit economics, payback windows, contribution margins, and 12-month performance trends across Shopify, Meta, Google, and other channels. We reconcile platform data against financial reality to understand where scale is real, inflated, or eroding profit.

Step 3 - Receive a Recorded Strategic Walkthrough

You receive a 30–60 minute recorded walkthrough that lays out what we found, what's blocking scale, and where your next best moves live. It's specific, tied to your economics, and designed to create immediate clarity and alignment.

Step 4 - Meet with our founder

You meet directly with our founder to pressure-test the conclusions, ask the hard questions, and talk through trade-offs. If there's a strong fit, we'll map what working together would look like. If not, you still leave with a clear direction and the reasoning behind it.

Not a campaign critique, ad account audit or salespitch in disguise.

It's a way to determine if you're ready for scaling and if not, what to do to get there.
