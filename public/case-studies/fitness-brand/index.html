FROM CHALLENGES TO SUCCESS

Celebrity-Endorsed, Algorithm-Starved

This leading fitness recovery brand had everything going for it: celebrity endorsements from Hollywood stars and athletes, placement in every location of a worldwide gym chain, and pioneer status in its product category. But their Google Ads account had hit a hard ceiling. Performance Max was cannibalizing branded search, the account was cluttered with redundant campaigns bidding against each other, and there was no segmentation between Search, Shopping, and Video. The result: an expensive, high-ticket product stuck at a scale that didn’t match the brand’s potential.

Challenges

The “Black Box” Scaling Limit

The account had become over-complicated and under-managed. By allowing Google’s automation to make all the decisions, the brand was losing the granular control needed to sell an expensive, technical product.

  • PMax misallocation: Performance Max was failing to utilize the Shopping network effectively. Instead of visual product placement, it was funneling the majority of the budget into Search, where costs-per-click were higher and competition was fiercer.
  • Redundant structures: The account was cluttered with duplicated and non-performing campaigns. This “bloat” was splitting the data and causing different campaigns to bid against one another, driving up acquisition costs.
  • Scaling bottleneck: Without clear segmentation between networks (Search vs. Shopping vs. Video), the brand couldn’t identify which lever to pull to increase volume without tanking efficiency.

Solution

Deconstructing the Machine for Precision Scale

We moved away from a “set it and forget it” automated approach and returned to a manual, high-control architecture designed for high-ticket sales.

1

Cutting the Fat

We conducted a radical account review, pausing redundant campaigns and eliminating wasted spend on non-performing assets. This immediately consolidated the data and cleaned up the account’s signal.

2

Manual Network Control

We moved beyond the PMax black box. We rebuilt dedicated, manual campaigns for Search and Shopping, allowing us to optimize the product feed for high-intent shoppers and craft specific messaging for high-ticket buyers.

3

YouTube for Top-of-Funnel

High-ticket items require education and social proof. We scaled YouTube campaigns to introduce the product to new audiences, building desire before they even reached the Search bar.

4

Feed Optimization

We overhauled the product feed to ensure the brand appeared for the most relevant, high-value technical queries, eliminating wasted spend on irrelevant searches.

Results

Consistent 5x Efficiency at Scale

By moving to a more intentional, segmented structure, we didn’t just grow the account. We transformed its role in the client’s business, making Google Ads a primary driver of brand expansion.

  • 300% account scale over a two-year period while maintaining performance
  • 170% year-over-year revenue growth from Google Ads
  • Consistent 5x+ ROI maintained even while scaling aggressively into cold traffic and YouTube
