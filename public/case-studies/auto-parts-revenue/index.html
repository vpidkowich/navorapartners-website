Client Case Study

A Journey of Restructuring Growth

This auto parts brand had a strong product catalog and loyal customer base, but their paid media was running on autopilot. All products were crammed into a single campaign with no differentiation between audiences, creative angles, or funnel stages. The result: rising acquisition costs, stagnant revenue, and no visibility into what was actually working.

"We knew we were leaving money on the table, but we didn't know where. Navora came in and showed us exactly what was broken and how to fix it."

Brand Owner

Auto Parts Ecommerce Brand

Challenges

Inventory Trapped in a Single Campaign

The brand was running their entire product catalog through a single shopping campaign with no segmentation. This created a cascade of problems that compounded over time, making it nearly impossible to scale efficiently.

  • No campaign structure: Thousands of SKUs competing for the same budget with no priority tiers, product grouping, or performance-based segmentation.
  • Blind spending: No separation between prospecting and retargeting meant the brand couldn't tell which dollars were acquiring new customers vs. recapturing existing ones.
  • Rising nCAC: Without guardrails, customer acquisition costs climbed steadily while revenue plateaued, squeezing margins and limiting reinvestment capacity.
  • Creative stagnation: The same ad creative had been running for months with no testing framework, leading to audience fatigue and declining click-through rates.

Solution

A Full-Funnel Restructuring

We didn't just optimize the existing setup; we rebuilt the entire paid media architecture from the ground up. The goal was to create a system that could scale predictably while keeping acquisition costs within profitable thresholds.

1

Campaign Architecture

Rebuilt from a single campaign into a structured multi-campaign setup with distinct product groupings, priority tiers, and budget allocation rules based on margin and volume potential.

2

Audience Segmentation

Separated prospecting from retargeting with distinct budgets, bid strategies, and creative assets. This gave the brand clear visibility into true new customer acquisition costs.

3

Creative Testing

Implemented a systematic creative testing framework with structured A/B tests, rotating angles, and performance-based creative scoring for continuous optimization.

4

nCAC Guardrails

Replaced arbitrary ROAS goals with P&L-aligned nCAC targets, giving the brand a profitability-first scaling framework that connected ad spend directly to business outcomes.

Results

Scaling with Confidence

The restructuring delivered results within the first month. As the new architecture matured and the creative testing framework produced winning assets, growth accelerated exponentially. The brand went from questioning whether paid media worked to making it their primary growth channel.
